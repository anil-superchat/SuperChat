Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can we get started?

Most customers can start testing SuperChat within 24-48 hours. For browser-based deployment, it's immediate. For on-premise installations, timeline depends on your infrastructure, but typically 1-2 weeks including training.

What deployment options are available?

We offer browser-based (WebGPU), on-premise (your infrastructure), private cloud (AWS/Azure/GCP), and hybrid deployment models. Our team will help you choose the best option for your requirements.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes! All enterprise customers receive dedicated technical support, including onboarding, training, and ongoing assistance. We offer email, phone, and Slack channel support based on your plan.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for the browser-based version with access to all models except GOD MODE. For enterprise trials with full features, contact our sales team.

What about data security and privacy?

SuperChat is designed with privacy first. On-premise deployments ensure zero external data transfer. We're GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2 compliant. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.

Can I integrate with our existing systems?

Absolutely! SuperChat includes 55+ workflow nodes for integrations including databases, cloud storage, email, APIs, and more. We also offer custom integration development for enterprise customers.
