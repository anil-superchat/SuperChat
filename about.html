Our Story

How SuperChat Was Born

2023

The Problem

We saw enterprises struggling with AI adoption. They wanted powerful AI capabilities but couldn't accept sending sensitive data to external APIs. Cloud-based AI services posed compliance risks, data sovereignty concerns, and unpredictable costs.

2024

The Breakthrough

WebGPU and WebLLM technologies matured, enabling browser-based AI inference with hardware acceleration. We realized we could build enterprise-grade AI that runs entirely on-premise, with zero external dependencies.

2025

The Solution

SuperChat was founded with a clear mission: deliver powerful AI models that organizations can deploy on their own infrastructure. We built a platform combining 7 specialized models with a visual workflow builder and 55+ integration nodes.

2026

Today & Beyond

SuperChat now powers AI workflows for enterprises across healthcare, finance, government, and regulated industries. We continue innovating, adding more models, improving performance, and expanding integration capabilities.
